Bio

I am a Member of Technical Staff at Microsoft AI. I received my PhD from LTI of CMU SCS in 2025, advised by Graham Neubig, on AI agents and code generation. I received my BS and MS in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2016 and 2019, advised by Kenny Q. Zhu.

Research

My current research is about large language models for long horizon agents. The eventual goal is to enable users to use natural language to instruct and interact with the computers in the future.
