Bio

I am currently a Master Candidate of Computer Science at ADAPT Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University since 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Kenny Q. Zhu. Prior to that, I obtained B.Eng of Computer Science from IEEE Honored Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and earned Best Thesis Award in SJTU Bachelor's Thesis Defense (Top 1%). I had several research internships in labs like ADAPT Lab, SJTU IIOT and OMNILAB during my undergraduate study in SJTU, as well as industry software development internships.

Research

I work in the intersection of natural language processing, text mining, information retrieval, machine learning and artificial intelligence. I had also done projects regarding urban computing, spatio-temporal data mining, computational social sciences, etc.

I'm a member of the Advanced Data And Programming Technology Lab (ADAPT-Lab) .
